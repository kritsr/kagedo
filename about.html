The Way of the Shadow

It is said that the path to Heaven's Gate is through the Way of the Shadows. Seven mystical spells hidden away from the world in the blood of destined warriors and the secret root of conflict and war for thousands of generations. Attaining all Seven Shadows grants ascension to a chosen warrior with the promise of immortality and the ability to move through worlds. However, Shadows can only be gathered by spilling the blood of those holding the spell.
